Virginia Tech Gaming Project

The VTGP is a student run group that focuses on teaching and encouraging VT students to design and develop their own video games. It meets every Tuesday and holds game jams for members to work together to develop a game in a short period of time. All experience levels of game design and coding are welcome, and make sure to always have fun!
